McLemore Auction Company, LLC is headquartered in Nashville, TN. Since it was founded
in 2006, the company has sold real estate and personal property throughout the Southeastern
United States.

McLemore Auction Company, LLC specializes in the sale of complex and valuable real estate
in Tennessee and surrounding states. It delivers excellent results to its clients through careful
resale evaluation and data driven marketing.

Recent auction highlights include the sale of the Stoneybrook subdivision in Antioch, TN for
$4.4 millon, the sale of an 820 acre cotton farm in Cowan, TN for $2.7 million and the
complete liquidation of the Canyon West Golf Course and Mickle's Valley View Ranch in
Weatherford, TX for $4.8 million.
